How capital is allocated and enforced across the lead marketplace.

Budget Architecture & Hierarchy

The system employs a multi-level budget structure, allowing for granular control from the company level all the way down to specific geographic locations. Budgets are assigned to categories based on lead type.

The 30-Day Rolling Window

Unlike traditional systems with hard monthly resets, this engine uses a dynamic 30-day rolling window for budget calculations. This means usage is always based on the most recent performance data, providing a more accurate and fair assessment. The calculation is also timezone-aware, respecting the company's local business day.

Budget Enforcement & Overage

The system provides flexible enforcement. By default, campaigns can slightly exceed their daily budget up to a 115% threshold, allowing for minor fluctuations. However, companies can opt for a "Never Exceed Budget" setting for strict, 100% enforcement.

This enforcement is applied primarily during BRS campaign filtering, where any campaign exceeding its limit is removed from consideration for new leads.
